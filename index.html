About Me

I am a research scholar in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India. I received my B.Tech degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from National Institute of Technology Warangal, India in 2011. Worked as an Engineering Officer in Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. for a period of two years where I handled projects for automating and modernizing retail outlets. My research interests include optimization of power system operations, operation and control of distribution systems, and microgrids.
