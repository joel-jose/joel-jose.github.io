About Me

I am a recent Ph. D. graduate from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India. I worked with Prof. Anupama Kowli on optimal design and operation of power distribution systems.

My research interests include:

  • Power distribution system operations
  • Optimization
  • Graph algorithms
  • Reliability and resilience in electric networks
